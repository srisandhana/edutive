Lesson Plan Assessment Rubric
Assessment Activity: Lesson Plan
Description: For all Grade
Criteria Score Description
Can write answers to questions Has adhered to the format given by the school; throughout the plan.
Objectives/ Outcomes Three- four objectives are not specific and do not relate to skills or concepts. Three to four objectives do not relate to the highest level of proficiency. One or two objectives are not measurable and time bound.
Assessment The assessments align to most of the objectives and reflect a variety of methods.
Instructional Strategies Plan of instruction is vague. Only the content that will be taught is given. The methodology is missing in more than two instances.
Resources Materials and resources are listed with specific citation in most cases. A range of resources and technological tools are used.
Differentiation Plans for accommodating learner differences are not included in the plan.
Home Assignments/ Extended Learning Activities A variety of home assignments planned. These support the attainment of the learning objectives.
