Written Test
General Instructions
  1. i. The examination shall consist of 100 (For Full Marks 80) Multiple Choice (MCQ), carrying 01 mark each.
  2. ii. Each MCQ will have 4 choices and a student shall mark his/her choice for MCQs on the system itself.
  3. iii. Each student can attempt 80 (For 80 Marks) number of questions randomly selected from the question bank out of 100.
  4. iv. A Total Test Duration is 120 Minutes.
  5. v. There will be no Negative Marking for any wrong answer or non-attempt, and answers will be auto calculated.
  6. vi. You can submit your test whenever you have completed the test. However, if the test time elapses, the system will automatically Submit .
  7. vii. Do not close any window directly when you are taking the test.
  8. viii. Select the appropriate answer for each question. Then click "Next" button to move to the Next Question & "Back" for previous question.
  9. ix. You can Bookmark any number of Questions by clicking on "Review" icon; you can remove the Bookmark by clicking on it again.
  10. x. You can change your response (Answer) for any question before submitting the test by marking the new response(Answer) (Radio Button). You can clear your response(Answer)

    for any question by using the "Clear" button to mark the Question unanswered.
  11. xi. Every time you select an option or a particular question, following changes can be seen on Question Panel.
    • Current
    • Answered
    • Unanswered
    • Not Viewed
    • Review
